Motivation

The evolution to 5G is expected to be the enabler for new types of services, which will reshape our society. Self-driving cars, artificial intelligence will be part of our daily life to mention some examples. of above major dynamics. As such this evolution will bring new opportunities for businesses making it possible to reposition Tunisian companies as players, which can benefit from opportunities worldwide. In parallel the social impact will be very important. Being in a completely passive role risks to impact our social well being. The question here is how we can correlate the GDP with other development measures like the Happiness Index. It is key that we build a focus group, which can play an active role in shaping our future in the light.
